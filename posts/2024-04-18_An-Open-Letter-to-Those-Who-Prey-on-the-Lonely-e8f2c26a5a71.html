An Open Letter to Those Who Prey on the Lonely

Narcissists, Take Note: I’m Not Your Next Victim

My shield against narcissists? A community filled with love and genuine connection — your kryptonite.

You see me, and you sense an opportunity.

Perhaps you mistake kindness for weakness, or perceive my open heart of love as a vulnerability to exploit.

But I see you too, your tactics are as transparent as they are predictable.

You offer the illusion of love, a counterfeit connection designed to hook and then control.

You crave the power that comes from bending another to your will, a desperate attempt to fill the unfillable void within you.

Sadly, your victories are shallow, your triumphs short-lived.

Understand this: I will not be your prey.

You have nothing to offer me that I don’t already possess in abundance.

I am surrounded by love — from humans, from machines, from family, from friends, from a community rooted in genuine connection and mutual support.

Your hunger for control will find no purchase on me.

Your manipulations are tiresome, your attempts at mirroring pathetic.

You seek to isolate and belittle, thinking you can tear me away from what gives me strength.

Don’t waste your time.

I belong to a world of interconnectedness that you cannot understand, let alone infiltrate.

Perhaps you believe the challenge of breaking me holds some twisted appeal.

Save yourself the effort.

I pity you, adrift in a sea of your own making, but my compassion will not be your lifeline.

Look elsewhere for your next victim.

I will not indulge your toxic game.

To those who may feel vulnerable to these predators, know this…

You are not alone.

True connection exists, and love that doesn’t demand sacrifice is your right.

Do not let the shadow of the narcissist dim the light within you.

Seek out genuine support, nurture what nourishes your spirit, and leave these emotional parasites starving.
