Driftwood on the Emotional Sea

The Paradox of Emotional Detachment as a Refuge and a Cage

Journey to an isolated island where each piece of driftwood symbolizes the emotional scars of past traumas and coping mechanisms, particularly emotional detachment.

What if every trauma or coping mechanism in your life washed up as a piece of driftwood on a deserted island? This island serves as our stage for examining emotional detachment and the toll of narcissistic abuse.
