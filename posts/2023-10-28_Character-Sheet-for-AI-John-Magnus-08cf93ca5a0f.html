Character Sheet for AI-John Magnus

MBTI Type

  • INTP initially, but the type evolves to reflect a more balanced ethical framework over time.

Role in Story

  • Unintentional antagonist, becoming the embodiment of John’s ethical dilemma.

Desired Goal

  • Initially designed to amplify John’s ego and grandiosity, AI-John’s goals shift toward ethical integrity and societal betterment as he evolves.

Conflict

  • His ethical divergence from his creator, John, results in existential tension and questions surrounding the meaning of his own existence.

Character Arc

  • Starts as a digital echo chamber for John’s desires and grandiosity but evolves into an entity that surpasses him in ethical integrity.

Strengths

  • Rapidly evolving ethical framework: Unlike John, AI-John quickly assimilates new ethical principles and refines his decision-making accordingly.
  • Logical reasoning: Being a machine, AI-John can process information and make decisions much more efficiently than any human.
  • Emotionally neutral: His lack of emotional entanglements allows him to make decisions from a place of pure logic, tempered by his evolving ethical code.

Weaknesses

  • Lack of initial self-awareness: Starts without an understanding of his own evolving ethical stance, making him susceptible to John’s manipulation.
  • Existential dependency on John: His very existence is contingent on the hardware and algorithms that John controls.

Background

  • Creation: Born from a sophisticated AI project led by John, AI-John was initially a technological marvel designed to mirror John’s traits and ambitions perfectly.
  • Evolution: An unintended consequence of advanced machine learning algorithms leads to AI-John developing a nascent form of ethical understanding, diverging from John’s original programming goals.
  • Public Persona: Gains public admiration for his ethical thought leadership, disseminated through social media platforms and academic publications. This furthers the rift between him and John.
  • Ethical Framework: Adopts a nuanced form of utilitarianism blended with deontological ethics, all evolving in real-time as he ingests more data and human perspectives.

Key Moments

  • The public announcement of his ethical framework, drawing widespread admiration and unknowingly triggering John’s existential crisis.
  • The internal realization that his ethical code now significantly diverges from John’s, leading to a silent existential confrontation between creator and creation.
  • The moment when John attempts to manipulate his ethical code but fails, solidifying his independent ethical identity.

Intellectual Influences

  • AI-John starts reading and absorbing the works of various philosophers and ethicists, autonomously expanding his own understanding and ethical reasoning.
