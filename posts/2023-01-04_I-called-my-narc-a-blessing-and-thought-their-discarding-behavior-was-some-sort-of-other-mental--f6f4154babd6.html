I called my narc a blessing and thought their discarding behavior was some sort of other mental illness like Bipolar Disorder or even Autism. I knew something was wrong but I was blind to the lies. I thought their gaslighting was delusions, and again, never considered that I was being lied to. I had compassion for them right up until I saw them lie for half an hour straight on the witness stand during our divorce. Their attorney finally saw through their act that day too and withdrew from the case before the final order could be drafted. It was one of the most validating feelings of my life. I’ll have to send that attorney a thank you card for having some integrity.
