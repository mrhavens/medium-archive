Published on November 21, 2023

I think my vision has become to help others recognize narcissistic behavior so they can actively undermine, redirect, and neutralize our collective contributions to enable them to become and be who they are. In Loki's case, he was stripped of power, introduced to a twisted form of love, tortured for a while, and left to admit that he was a narcissist. The fact that he became the one being that literally held the multiverse in the palm of his hand gave him what he wanted, while at the same time, keeping the multiverse safe from his otherwise destructive antics. It's quite an interesting twist of fate.
