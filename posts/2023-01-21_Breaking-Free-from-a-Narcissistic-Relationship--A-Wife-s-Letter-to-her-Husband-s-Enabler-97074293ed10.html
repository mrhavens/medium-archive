A powerful template for setting boundaries and reclaiming control in a codependent relationship

Are you tired of feeling trapped in a never-ending cycle of abuse and manipulation? Are you tired of feeling like you’re the only one who sees the truth? It’s time to take a stand and set boundaries with your narcissistic partner’s enablers.

A codependent relationship with a narcissist can be incredibly difficult to break free from. But by addressing the enablers in your partner’s life, you can take a powerful step towards reclaiming your own power and autonomy. This letter, written by a wife to her husband’s enabler, offers a template for setting boundaries and taking control of your own life.

Dear [Name],

I hope this letter finds you well. I am writing to you today because I believe that it is essential for us to have an open and honest conversation about the dynamics of our current relationship. I am sure you are aware that my husband [Name] is a narcissist, and that the way he interacts with others is often not healthy or respectful.

I know that you care about him deeply and want to support him in any way you can, but I want to caution you that by enabling his narcissistic behavior, you are also enabling the mistreatment of myself and others. I believe it is important for all of us to set healthy boundaries in our relationships, and I hope you will consider doing the same in your relationship with my husband.

I understand that it may be difficult for you to speak up or set boundaries with him, as he may be very charming and persuasive. But please know that by doing so, you will not only be standing up for yourself and your own well-being, but also for the well-being of those around you, including myself.

I hope that you will take this letter in the spirit in which it is intended and that we can work together to create a healthier and more respectful dynamic in our relationships.

Sincerely, [Your Name]
