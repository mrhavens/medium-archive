Love Bombing: The Manipulative Tactic Used by Narcissists

From romantic relationships to cults and businesses, understanding the signs and consequences of this insidious behavior

Have you ever been in a relationship where you were showered with love and affection, only to later realize that it was all a ploy to win you over and manipulate you? This tactic, known as love bombing, is often used by individuals with narcissistic tendencies in order to gain control and power over their targets. In this article, we’ll delve deeper into the definition and consequences of love bombing, and how it can occur in any type of relationship, from romantic to professional.

Love bombing is a tactic often used by individuals with narcissistic tendencies in order to win over and manipulate their targets. While it is most commonly associated with romantic or sexual relationships, it can also occur in other types of relationships, such as friendships, familial relationships, and even professional or business relationships.

In these cases, the narcissist may use similar tactics, such as excessive flattery, attention, and gifts, in order to gain control and manipulate their target. Love bombing can even be found in the recruiting and retention tactics of cults and other social groups, as well as in the marketing and customer service strategies of some businesses.

It is important to be aware that love bombing does not always have to occur within the context of a romantic or sexual relationship, and that it can happen in any type of relationship. If you suspect that you or someone you know is being love bombed, it is important to pay attention to red flags and to seek the advice of a trusted therapist, friend, or family member.
