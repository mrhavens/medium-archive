The Silent Treatment: Understanding and Coping with Emotional Manipulation

Navigating the painful and confusing world of being ignored

Imagine being in a relationship with someone who suddenly stops speaking to you. They refuse to acknowledge your existence, and the silence is deafening. This is the world of the silent treatment — a form of emotional manipulation that can leave you feeling confused, hurt, and alone.
