Creating Believable Characters: A Comprehensive Character Sheet Template

Learn how to develop fully realized characters for your writing.

When writing a fictional character, it’s important to create a fully realized and well-rounded individual. A character sheet template can be a helpful tool in this process, as it allows you to systematically gather and organize information about your character’s identity, development, interpersonal dynamics, external factors, and inner world.

By creating a character sheet, you can get a comprehensive overview of your character and ensure that they are consistent and believable within the context of your story. This can also help you to identify any gaps or inconsistencies in your character’s background or personality and can serve as a reference as you write your story. Overall, using a character sheet can help you to create rich and complex characters that will bring your story to life.

Character sheets are especially important for main characters, such as the protagonist and the villain, as they will be central to the story and will need to be fully developed in order to be believable and engaging.

The template provided below is a good starting point for developing a character and includes sections on identity, development, interpersonal dynamics, external factors, and inner world.

By using this character sheet as a template for your own characters, you will have a comprehensive overview of your character, including their appearance, personality, background, goals, motivations, and relationships.

Once complete, this information can then be used as a reference as you write the story, ensuring that the character remains consistent and believable.

While a character sheet is a useful tool for getting started with character development, it is important to keep in mind that a more robust and fully developed character bible may be needed for main characters.

A character bible is a more detailed and comprehensive record of a character, including everything from their physical appearance and personality to their past experiences and future goals. By creating a character bible, you can ensure that your main characters are fully realized, are well-rounded, and have enormous depth, while serving as a reference for maintaining consistency and believability throughout the story.
