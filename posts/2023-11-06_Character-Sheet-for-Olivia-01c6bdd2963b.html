Character Sheet for Olivia

Name: Olivia

Age: Mid 30s

Physical Description: Warm hazel eyes and chestnut hair reminiscent of Ethan’s features. Stylishly dressed with an air of confidence about her.

MBTI: ENFJ — The Protagonist Personality

As an ENFJ, Olivia is:

  • Warm, empathetic, and insightful of others’ emotions
  • Encouraging and devoted to supporting loved ones
  • Natural mentor who helps others grow and thrive
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Sees and nurtures others’ potential
  • Passionate about causes aligned with her values
  • Natural leader who unites people
  • Can become overly involved in others’ lives

Backstory:

  • Ethan’s older sister by a few years
  • They had a stable, loving childhood growing up together
  • As kids, Olivia would encourage Ethan’s creativity and sensitivity
  • Very protective of her little brother, especially after their parents passed
  • Got married and settled down before Ethan met Maya
  • Was happy to see Ethan so in love, though sensed issues in his relationship
  • Tried to be there for Ethan when Maya left him
  • Worried about Ethan retreating from life and dwelling on his lost love
  • Reached out often, offering comfort and guidance through his grief
  • Pushed Ethan to open up about his struggles and move forward
  • Remained a steady, supportive presence in his life
