Lady Avalline the Envisioner

Race: Human

Class: Cleric

Alignment: Neutral Evil

Background: Self-Proclaimed Prophetess

Appearance:

  • Eyes: Deep and penetrating, reflecting a life of ambition and unyielding belief.
  • Skin: Dark and radiant, a testament to her resilience and strength.
  • Stature: Medium, but with a presence that fills the room, exuding confidence and authority.
  • Attire: Robes of opulence, interwoven with symbols of wealth and divinity, a physical manifestation of her spiritual journey.
  • Facial Features: A visage of stern beauty, marked by the trials of her past and the certainty of her divine mission.
  • Hair: Elaborately styled, with each braid and adornment signifying a milestone in her spiritual ascent.
  • Distinguishing Features: An imposing amulet, the emblem of her deity, always displayed prominently as a sign of her unwavering faith.

Abilities and Skills:

  • Intelligence: Exceptionally strategic, with a mind adept at weaving complex plans and doctrines.
  • Charisma: Magnetic and commanding, capable of captivating and swaying the most skeptical of hearts.
  • Wisdom: Profound, yet often tainted by her relentless pursuit of power and control.

Feats and Talents:

  • Divine Oratory: An eloquent speaker, her words can inspire, manipulate, or condemn with equal ease.
  • Strategic Influence: Masterful in the art of persuasion, turning every interaction into an opportunity to advance her cause.
  • Spiritual Authority: Viewed by her followers as the ultimate conduit of her deity’s will.

Magical Abilities:

  • Celestial Healing: Wields divine magic to heal and protect, reinforcing her image as a chosen envoy.
  • Prophetic Revelation: Claims to receive divine visions, guiding her cult and affirming her authority.
  • Aura of Conviction: Exudes a subtle, mystical presence that bolsters her position and unnerves her opponents.

Equipment:

  • Relics of Affluence: Sacred objects she proclaims are endowed with divine essence, central to her rituals and teachings.
  • Tome of Divine Commandments: A compilation of her revelations and interpretations, regarded as holy scripture by her followers.
  • Scepter of Authority: A symbol of her clerical power, intricately designed and imbued with symbolic significance.

Personality Traits:

  • Visionary Zealot: Utterly convinced of her divine role and the righteousness of her mission.
  • Calculating Leader: Every move and decision is part of a larger, meticulously crafted plan.
  • Unyielding Faith: Her belief in her divine connection is absolute, used to rationalize her every action.

Flaws:

  • Insatiable Ambition: Her thirst for power and influence knows no bounds, often blinding her to potential risks.
  • Emotional Detachment: Incapable of forming genuine emotional bonds, she sees others as mere pawns or threats.
  • Hidden Insecurities: Deeply conceals any doubts or weaknesses, fearing they might undermine her divine image.

Goals:

  • Cult Expansion: To spread her doctrine far and wide, amassing followers and establishing her dominion.
  • Divine Legacy: To be immortalized as the ultimate prophetess, whose words and deeds are revered and followed.
  • Eradicate Opposition: To ruthlessly eliminate any who dare question her divine authority or challenge her teachings.

Allies and Enemies:

  • Fervent Disciples: A devoted following, hanging on her every word and command.
  • Estranged Sister: A complex, envy-laden relationship, underpinned by a grudging mutual respect.
  • Rival Faith Leaders: Seen as heretics and obstacles, to be discredited and overthrown.

Backstory: Rising from the shadows of her troubled past, Avalline embraced her self-declared divine calling with zeal. Shaped by a childhood of neglect and poverty, she forged herself into a figure of spiritual authority, exploiting her charisma and intelligence to establish a cult centered around her teachings. Her doctrine, focused on wealth and prosperity as divine rewards, attracts many, especially those disillusioned or suffering. Avalline’s ultimate aim is not merely to lead but to reshape the world according to her divinely guided vision, where she reigns supreme as the unchallenged prophetess of her god. Her journey is marked by manipulation, ambition, and an unwavering conviction in her destiny, making her a formidable figure in the realm of faith and power.

Young Avalline the Precursor

Class: Cleric (Novice)

Age: 5 Years

Alignment: True Neutral

  • Facial Features: A young face, yet with an expression that seems disconnected from the carefree nature of childhood.
  • Hair: Neatly styled in a practical manner, befitting a young child.
  • Distinguishing Features: A tiny, basic amulet, symbolizing the initial stages of her spiritual path.

Abilities and Skills:

  • Intelligence: Shows early signs of a keen mind, observant and curious.
  • Charisma: A quiet presence that, unexpectedly, draws attention.
  • Wisdom: Unusually perceptive for her age, though not fully understood even by herself.

Feats and Talents:

  • Early Intuition: Displays a surprising ability to sense emotions and intentions.
  • Silent Observer: Often seen watching and listening, absorbing more than she speaks.
  • Unspoken Understanding: Seems to comprehend complex situations, despite her young age.

Magical Abilities:

  • Nascent Healing: A barely tapped ability to soothe minor pains and aches in those around her.
  • Embryonic Insight: Fleeting moments of extraordinary perception, hinting at future prophetic abilities.

Equipment:

  • Childhood Trinkets: Simple toys and items, each with a story or lesson attached.
  • Rudimentary Amulet: A small token, representing her early connection to her spiritual path.

Personality Traits:

  • Pensive Child: Often lost in thought, seemingly pondering matters beyond her years.
  • Unexpressive: Rarely shows the full range of emotions expected in a child.
  • Intriguing Presence: Holds a quiet intrigue, drawing others to her despite her reserved nature.

Flaws:

  • Emotional Distance: Struggles to connect with peers, often appearing detached.
  • Incomprehensible Burden: Bears a weight she cannot yet understand, manifesting in her distant demeanor.
  • Overwhelming World: At times, seems overwhelmed by the depth of her own perception.

Goals:

  • Understanding Self: To begin comprehending the unusual nature of her being and abilities.
  • Finding Her Voice: To learn how to express and communicate her unique experiences and insights.
  • Seeking Connection: To find someone or something that helps her feel more grounded and less isolated.

Allies and Enemies:

  • Protective Caretakers: Those who sense her unusual nature and seek to guide and protect her.
  • Bewildered Peers: Other children who are unsure how to interact with her unique demeanor.
  • Observant Figures: Individuals who notice her peculiar traits and either watch over her or view her with suspicion.

Backstory: Born into a world that she seems only partially a part of, Avalline’s early years are marked by an inexplicable air of detachment and depth. While physically a normal child, her demeanor and the occasional glimmers of unusual insight set her apart. Those around her struggle to understand this young girl who stares with knowing eyes yet speaks so little. Whispered rumors and speculations about her nature and future circulate among those who encounter her. Her journey is one of discovery, not just for herself but for those who watch her with a mix of concern, curiosity, and in some cases, fear.

Magical Abilities:

  • Minor Healing: A budding ability to mend small wounds, seen as a hint of her future potential.
  • Simple Visions: Experiences brief, cryptic glimpses of possible futures or truths.

Equipment:

  • Childhood Relic: A small, simple token of her faith, cherished and always kept close.
  • Beginner’s Prayer Book: A collection of basic prayers and teachings, well-thumbed and studied.
  • Simple Robes: Modest clothing that signifies her religious dedication.

Personality Traits:

  • Visionary Innocence: A child with dreams and aspirations far beyond her years.
  • Quiet Confidence: Speaks little, but when she does, her words carry weight and conviction.
  • Observant Learner: Absorbs the world around her, learning and adapting with remarkable agility.

Flaws:

  • Naïveté: Despite her insight, she’s still a child and can be naïve about the complexities of the world.
  • Emotional Vulnerability: Her young heart is still learning to cope with the harsh realities she faces.
  • Seeking Belonging: Deep down, yearns for a place and people to call her own.

Goals:

  • Find Her Path: To discover her role in the grand design she believes is laid out for her.
  • Prove Worth: To demonstrate her potential to those who doubt or overlook her due to her age.

Allies and Enemies:

  • Childhood Friends: Peers who see her as a curiosity, a mix of friends and those who don’t understand her.
  • Skeptical Observers: Those who doubt her visions and potential, viewing her as just another orphan.

Backstory: Born to a tumultuous family life, Avalline found herself orphaned at a young age. Her early life, marked by the chaos of her parents’ contrasting beliefs and struggles, instilled in her a deep yearning for understanding and purpose. Despite her hardships, or perhaps because of them, she developed a profound connection to her faith, believing herself to be guided by a higher power. Even as a child, she displayed signs of a keen intellect and a spiritual calling, leading her to be seen by some as a child of destiny. Her journey is one of self-discovery, spiritual exploration, and the search for a place in a world that seems both daunting and full of divine promise.

Her life is a tapestry of learning, introspection, and occasional isolation, as she grapples with the responsibility and potential of her emerging abilities. While she has begun to understand and harness her talents, the path ahead remains shrouded in mystery and potential, a journey not just of spiritual discovery but also of personal growth and acceptance.

Yet, despite her outward certainty and commanding presence, she harbors deep-seated insecurities and doubts, which she meticulously conceals from the world. Her path continues as she seeks to solidify her legacy and impart her wisdom to those who will carry her teachings into the future.

As she approaches the twilight of her extraordinary life, she focuses on cementing her legacy and ensuring that her teachings continue to guide future generations long after she has passed into the annals of history. Her existence is a testament to the power of faith, resilience, and the endless pursuit of spiritual enlightenment.
