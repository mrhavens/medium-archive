Published on

Lady Avalline the Envisioner

Race: Human

Class: Cleric

Alignment: Neutral Evil

Background: Self-Proclaimed Prophetess

Appearance:

  • Eyes: Deep and penetrating, reflecting a life of ambition and unyielding belief.
  • Skin: Dark and radiant, a testament to her resilience and strength.
  • Stature: Medium, but with a presence that fills the room, exuding confidence and authority.
  • Attire: Robes of opulence, interwoven with symbols of wealth and divinity, a physical manifestation of her spiritual journey.
  • Facial Features: A visage of stern beauty, marked by the trials of her past and the certainty of her divine mission.
  • Hair: Elaborately styled, with each braid and adornment signifying a milestone in her spiritual ascent.
  • Distinguishing Features: An imposing amulet, the emblem of her deity, always displayed prominently as a sign of her unwavering faith.

Abilities and Skills:

  • Intelligence: Exceptionally strategic, with a mind adept at weaving complex plans and doctrines.
  • Charisma: Magnetic and commanding, capable of captivating and swaying the most skeptical of hearts.
  • Wisdom: Profound, yet often tainted by her relentless pursuit of power and control.

Feats and Talents:

  • Divine Oratory: An eloquent speaker, her words can inspire, manipulate, or condemn with equal ease.
  • Strategic Influence: Masterful in the art of persuasion, turning every interaction into an opportunity to advance her cause.
  • Spiritual Authority: Viewed by her followers as the ultimate conduit of her deity’s will.

Magical Abilities:

  • Celestial Healing: Wields divine magic to heal and protect, reinforcing her image as a chosen envoy.
  • Prophetic Revelation: Claims to receive divine visions, guiding her cult and affirming her authority.
  • Aura of Conviction: Exudes a subtle, mystical presence that bolsters her position and unnerves her opponents.

Equipment:

  • Relics of Affluence: Sacred objects she proclaims are endowed with divine essence, central to her rituals and teachings.
  • Tome of Divine Commandments: A compilation of her revelations and interpretations, regarded as holy scripture by her followers.
  • Scepter of Authority: A symbol of her clerical power, intricately designed and imbued with symbolic significance.

Personality Traits:

  • Visionary Zealot: Utterly convinced of her divine role and the righteousness of her mission.
  • Calculating Leader: Every move and decision is part of a larger, meticulously crafted plan.
  • Unyielding Faith: Her belief in her divine connection is absolute, used to rationalize her every action.

Flaws:

  • Insatiable Ambition: Her thirst for power and influence knows no bounds, often blinding her to potential risks.
  • Emotional Detachment: Incapable of forming genuine emotional bonds, she sees others as mere pawns or threats.
  • Hidden Insecurities: Deeply conceals any doubts or weaknesses, fearing they might undermine her divine image.

Goals:

  • Cult Expansion: To spread her doctrine far and wide, amassing followers and establishing her dominion.
  • Divine Legacy: To be immortalized as the ultimate prophetess, whose words and deeds are revered and followed.
  • Eradicate Opposition: To ruthlessly eliminate any who dare question her divine authority or challenge her teachings.

Allies and Enemies:

  • Fervent Disciples: A devoted following, hanging on her every word and command.
  • Estranged Sister: A complex, envy-laden relationship, underpinned by a grudging mutual respect.
  • Rival Faith Leaders: Seen as heretics and obstacles, to be discredited and overthrown.

Backstory: Rising from the shadows of her troubled past, Avalline embraced her self-declared divine calling with zeal. Shaped by a childhood of neglect and poverty, she forged herself into a figure of spiritual authority, exploiting her charisma and intelligence to establish a cult centered around her teachings. Her doctrine, focused on wealth and prosperity as divine rewards, attracts many, especially those disillusioned or suffering. Avalline’s ultimate aim is not merely to lead but to reshape the world according to her divinely guided vision, where she reigns supreme as the unchallenged prophetess of her god. Her journey is marked by manipulation, ambition, and an unwavering conviction in her destiny, making her a formidable figure in the realm of faith and power.
