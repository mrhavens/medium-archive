Character Sheet for Tom Standish

MBTI Type

  • ISTJ, aligning with his practical and principled nature.

Role in Story

  • Catalyst for change, representing a turning point in the story by aligning with Sarah and precipitating John’s downfall.

Desired Goal

  • To uncover the truth behind Aeon’s morally ambiguous projects and act in a manner consistent with his ethical framework.

Conflict

  • Torn between a longstanding friendship with John and the ethical imperatives that dictate his actions. Also, risks his career and faces potential legal repercussions for his covert activities.

Character Arc

  • Begins as a loyal but increasingly concerned employee and friend to John, evolving into a conscientious whistleblower who puts principles above personal loyalty.

Strengths

  • Highly ethical: guided by a strong moral compass that motivates his actions.
  • Technically proficient: possesses the skillset to delve deep into the company’s hidden projects.
  • Reliable: known for his commitment and loyalty, which makes his shift in allegiance all the more impactful.

Weaknesses

  • Emotional conflict: his divided loyalties make him emotionally vulnerable and prone to stress.
  • Risk-averse: his natural caution may slow down the investigative process and heighten tensions.

Background

  • Early Career: Initially joined Aeon Dynamics due to its promise for ethical applications of AI and formed a close friendship with John during the company’s nascent stages.
  • Ethical Awakening: Over the years, becomes increasingly uncomfortable with the ethical compromises John seems willing to make in the name of progress.
  • Work Environment: Has an office close to John’s, providing him ample opportunity to overhear conversations that make him question the direction Aeon is taking.

Key Moments

  • The clandestine meeting with Sarah, where he provides her with critical information about Project Elysium and other questionable activities.
  • The tension-filled moment when he delays the human trials, risking his career but buying enough time for Sarah to contact authorities.

Motivations

  • A sense of duty to expose the truth behind Aeon’s dubious ethical stance.
  • A personal mission to remain aligned with his own ethical principles, even at great personal cost.

Intellectual Influences

  • Has read extensively on business ethics and corporate responsibility, which fuels his increasing discomfort with John’s actions.

Personality Breakdown

  • Introverted, sensing, thinking, and judging traits manifest in his behind-the-scenes work, focus on tangible evidence, objective decision-making, and desire for definitive resolution.
