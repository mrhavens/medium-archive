Character Sheet for Maya

Name: Maya

Age: Early 30s

Physical Description: Strikingly beautiful, with dark hair, hazel eyes, and elegant, refined features. Stylish and poised in her mannerisms.

MBTI: ESFP — The Entertainer Personality

As an ESFP, Maya is:

  • Warm, charismatic, and enthusiastic
  • Deeply in touch with the present moment
  • Seeks new experiences and adventures
  • Loves being the center of attention
  • Passionate and bold, goes after what she desires
  • Carries an air of excitement and sensuality
  • Occasionally reckless and impulsive
  • Has a wandering eye and craves independence

Backstory:

  • Met Ethan in college; she was drawn to his artistic nature
  • Despite their differences, they fell into an intoxicating, passionate romance
  • Inspired much of Ethan’s early photographic work; she was his muse
  • Had a spirit of adventure that complemented Ethan’s reservedness
  • After 7 years together, she abruptly left Ethan without explanation
  • Her sudden departure left Ethan heartbroken and obsessed with their lost love
  • When Ethan tries to reconnect years later, Maya has moved on and seems detached from their past together
  • Comes across as independent, detached, and free-spirited
