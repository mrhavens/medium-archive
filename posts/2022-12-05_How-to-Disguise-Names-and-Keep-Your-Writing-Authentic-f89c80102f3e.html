How to Disguise Names and Keep Your Writing Authentic

What to do when fictionalizing names in your writing

Do you want to make your story more engaging and captivating for readers?

Incorporating real people into your fiction writing can be a great way to bring your story to life, but how can you do this without compromising the authenticity of your story?

When writing fiction, using real people in your story can be a great way to bring it to life. However, it is important to ensure that you do this without compromising the authenticity of your story. To achieve this, consider making subtle changes to the people you include in your writing.

For example, you can change their physical characteristics, their personalities, or their backgrounds. This way, you can ensure that their presence in the story remains true to your vision while still keeping it grounded in reality.

When crafting a fictional story, it’s essential to be mindful of the details you include to ensure a realistic and memorable reading experience while avoiding any potential legal issues due to confusion between a fictional character and a real person.
