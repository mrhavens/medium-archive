Character Sheet for Sarah Revel

MBTI Type

  • INFJ

Role in Story

  • Tertiary protagonist

Desired Goal

  • To ensure that Aeon Dynamics’ projects adhere to the highest ethical standards.

Conflict

  • Torn between her professional ambition and her moral integrity; questions her ability to influence John’s ethics.

Character Arc

  • Starts as a hopeful consultant and ends as a determined whistleblower, leading to John’s downfall.

Strengths

  • Principled: Strong moral compass and commitment to ethical AI development.
  • Cautious: Takes time to investigate Aeon’s projects and doesn’t take things at face value.
  • Brave: Willing to risk her career and confront John to maintain her principles.
  • Strategic: Understands when to challenge John directly versus subtly undermining him to expose his unethical practices.

Weaknesses

  • Idealistic: Believes she can change John and Aeon Dynamics from the inside, which might not be realistic.
  • Risk-Averse: Initially hesitates to confront John and expose him due to the potential risk to her career.
  • Internal Conflict: Struggles between her sense of duty to whistleblow and the ramifications it could have on her professional life.

Background

  • Expert in machine learning and AI ethics with an established career and a reputation for integrity.
  • Recruited by John to consult on Aeon Dynamics’ projects.
  • Has always held strong ethical views about technology and its impact on society, reinforced by witnessing multiple instances of tech misuse in her career.

Key Scenes

  • Presses John to commit to ethical practices during their initial meeting.
  • Discovers animal testing data that Aeon Dynamics has been hiding.
  • Meets covertly with Tom Standish to share findings and plan next steps.
  • Notifies authorities about human trials once the evidence is in hand.
  • Confronts John, exposing his unethical experiments and triggering his downfall.
