This is still my position.

Published on November 30, 2022 at 02:16 AM

This is still my position. We don't need quantum anything to explain consciousness. But need doesn't dictate what is or isn't. If there's a reasonable argument that supports the possibility, dismissing it for lack of need isn't going to cause people to abandon the search for the possibility. I'll admit, quantum anything has been sexy for over a century. And that sexyness keeps pushing forward a lot of discovery, intended or unintended... be it needed or not.
