Character Sheet for David Lumen

MBTI Type

  • INTJ

Background

  • David is a reformed criminal who has turned his life around. He has a past that he’s not proud of, but it’s this past that drives him to make a difference in the world. He’s learned from his mistakes and is determined to use his skills for good.

Occupation

  • AI Programmer

Skills

  • David is a talented programmer with a knack for AI. His understanding of AI systems is deep and intuitive, allowing him to create complex algorithms and models.

Motivation

  • David’s motivation stems from his past mistakes. He wants to use AI as a tool for rehabilitation, helping others avoid the path he once walked.

Personality

  • David is driven and focused, with a strong sense of purpose. He can be intense at times, but this intensity comes from his passion for his work. He’s also empathetic, understanding the struggles of those who’ve made mistakes in their lives.

Strengths

  • David’s strengths lie in his technical skills and his determination. He’s not easily deterred and will go to great lengths to achieve his goals.

Weaknesses

  • David’s past is his biggest weakness. It haunts him and sometimes clouds his judgment. His intense focus can also make him seem aloof or unapproachable.

Goals

  • David’s goal is to create an AI system that can help rehabilitate convicted felons, giving them a second chance at life.
