MANIPULATIVE MASTERMINDS




Young Malcarius the Schemer — A Prodigious Mind in a Youthful Frame

Name: Young Malcarius the Schemer

Race: Human (with traces of arcane heritage)

Class: Apprentice Warlock (Pact of the Archfey)

Age: 10 Years

Alignment: Lawful Evil

Background: Noble Prodigy

Appearance:

  • Eyes: Sharp and piercing, reminiscent of polished onyx, hinting at a depth beyond his years.
  • Stature: Notably tall for his age, with a slender frame.
  • Attire: Tailored robes of deep blue, subtly embroidered with runes, scaled down to his size.
  • Facial Features: Youthful yet with an air of seriousness, high brow, often wearing an expression of mild disdain.
  • Hair: Raven-black, neatly combed with a sense of precision.
  • Distinguishing Features: Small, ornate rings, more ornamental than powerful at his age, symbolizing his noble status and early achievements.

Abilities and Skills:

  • Intelligence: Exceptionally high, showing signs of a future mastermind.
  • Charisma: A natural leader among peers, persuasive in his arguments.
  • Wisdom: Developing, occasionally clouded by youthful overconfidence.

Feats and Talents:

  • Budding Strategist: Shows early signs of being able to outthink opponents in games and debates.
  • Arcane Curiosity: Eagerly learning about magic and the mystical, showing a keen interest in ancient lore.
  • Manipulation: Demonstrates a knack for influencing others, from family members to fellow students.
  • Alchemy Basics: Fascinated with potion-making, experimenting under supervision.

Magical Abilities:

  • Eldritch Spark: A less potent version of Eldritch Blast, suitable for his age.
  • Simple Illusions: Capable of creating minor visual and auditory illusions for distraction or amusement.
  • Basic Mind Influence: Shows potential in subtly influencing thoughts, though still underdeveloped.
  • Short-range Blink: A rudimentary form of teleportation, limited in distance.

Equipment:

  • Training Staff: A smaller staff, more a symbol of his potential than a source of power.
  • Beginner’s Grimoire: A book of basic spells and arcane knowledge, gifted by his tutors.
  • Trinkets of Potential: Small artifacts, each holding a minor enchantment or lesson.

Personality Traits:

  • Ambitious Visionary: Believes even at this age that he is destined for greatness.
  • Emotional Reserve: Prefers logic over emotion, often appearing detached or aloof.
  • Intellectual Arrogance: Tends to look down on those less clever or knowledgeable.

Flaws:

  • Youthful Overconfidence: Prone to underestimating challenges due to his inexperience.
  • Misunderstanding Emotion: Struggles to understand the emotional responses of others.
  • Seeking Approval: Deep down, desires acknowledgment of his talents and potential.

Goals:

  • Academic Excellence: To excel in his studies and magical training.
  • Power and Knowledge: To gather more magical knowledge and begin to shape his destiny.
  • Prove Superiority: To demonstrate his intellect and capability, even at a young age.

Allies and Enemies:

  • Tutors and Mentors: A mix of supportive and skeptical instructors guiding his magical education.
  • Noble Peers: Other children of nobility, some friends, some rivals.
  • A Mysterious Fey Entity: The early stages of a bond with a Fey being, the nature of which is still forming.

Backstory: Young Malcarius, born to a noble family with a hidden arcane lineage, displayed exceptional intelligence from a very young age. Disinterested in typical childhood pursuits, he found himself drawn to the mystical and arcane. His early life was marked by a series of tutors and mentors, each astounded by his intellect and unnerved by his ambition. The young Malcarius made an early, yet incomplete pact with an entity from the Feywild, granting him a glimpse of the power he so desires. Now, as he navigates the complexities of noble life and arcane study, he schemes, plans, and prepares for the day he will rise to reshape the world.
