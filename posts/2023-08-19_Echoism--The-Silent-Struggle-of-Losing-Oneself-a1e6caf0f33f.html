NEUTRALIZING NARCISSISM

Echoism: The Silent Struggle of Losing Oneself

Understanding the Impact of Narcissistic Relationships on Personal Identity

A deep dive into Echoism, an often-overlooked condition that results from prolonged exposure to narcissistic individuals, leading to the erasure of personal identity and autonomy.

Echoism is more than just a psychological term; it’s a silent cry for recognition from those who have become invisible in the shadows of narcissistic relationships. As a concept, it intrigues, but as a reality, it devastates. Let’s explore this hidden affliction that traps people in a cycle of self-denial and suppressed identity.

Introduction

The name “Echoism” comes from the Greek myth of Echo, who could only repeat the words of others and lost her own voice. Similarly, individuals trapped in the grip of Echoism lose themselves while attending to the needs of a narcissistic partner. They become mirrors reflecting others and forgetting their own reflection. But what exactly is Echoism, and how does it manifest? Let’s unravel the mystery.
