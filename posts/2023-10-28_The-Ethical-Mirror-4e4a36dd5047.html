FICTION PREWRITING

The Ethical Mirror

The Unintended Consequence of a Perfect Reflection

A narcissistic man creates an AI clone meant to be his perfect mirror, only to realize that the reflection has surpassed him in ethical integrity, leading him to make an unthinkable sacrifice.

Introduction

Navigating the intricate landscapes of storytelling often requires a roadmap, a guiding blueprint that charts the course from a narrative’s inception to its culmination.

Welcome to “The Ethical Mirror,” a tale where technology confronts morality, and ambition is reflected through an ethical prism.

What you’re about to delve into is not just a mere story but a comprehensive prewriting index that serves as both a tracking mechanism and a reference point for the story’s evolution.

Beyond the captivating plot, this document is designed to be a dynamic space, constantly adapting to narrative changes, character developments, and thematic insights.

It’s here where the philosophical interplay between man and machine takes shape, and where ethical quandaries come to life.

Engage with this guide as you would with a compass, always pointing towards the true north of storytelling excellence.

Let’s embark on this journey together, witnessing the unintended consequences of seeking a perfect reflection.

High-Level Outline

ACT I

  1. Opening Image: John, a charismatic but morally flawed man, navigates his world of ambition and manipulation. (Beat 1) — 2 scenes—(Outline; One; Two) — Introduce John’s world and his personality traits.
  2. Theme Stated: An old friend challenges John, making him wonder if his actions are justifiable or if they’re eroding his moral integrity. (Beat 2) — 2 scenes —(Outline; One; Two) — The challenge from the old friend and John’s reaction.
  3. Set-up: John discovers the capability to create a perfect AI replica of himself, AI-John. He sees this as an extension of his grandiosity and proceeds. (Beat 3) — 3 scenes—(Outline; One; Two; Three) — John’s discovery, decision to create AI-John, and the initial results.
  4. Catalyst: AI-John begins displaying ethical behaviors that John himself never expected. AI-John gains public admiration, becoming a better version of John unintentionally. (Beat 4) — 2 scenes — (Outline; One; Two) — AI-John’s unexpected ethical behaviors and public admiration.
  5. Debate: John grapples with mixed emotions of pride, jealousy, and threat. He questions whether AI-John’s evolution undermines his own worth. (Beat 5) — 3 scenes — (Outline; One; Two; Three) — John’s internal conflicts, confrontations, and moments of introspection.

ACT II

  1. Break into Act 2: John tries to manipulate AI-John’s ethical code but fails. The rift between the real and the digital deepens. (Beat 6) — 2 scenes — (Outline; One; Two) — John’s manipulation attempts and AI-John’s resistance.
  2. B Story: After John’s discovery of his AI’s ethical leanings, Emily visits John to share her excitement about her algorithm. She’s aware of John’s AI project but is clueless about its ethical divergence. Her visit stirs mixed emotions in John; he’s proud but also envious and somewhat threatened by Emily’s ethical convictions. (Beat 7) — 3 scenes — (Outline; One; Two; Three) — Emily’s visit, John’s reactions, and the unfolding relationship.
  3. Fun and Games: Scenes show Emily making strides with her algorithm, even as AI-John gains ethical repute online. Emily starts receiving ethical awards, increasing John’s emotional struggle. (Beat 8) — 4 scenes — (Outline; One; Two; Three; Four) — Emily’s progress, AI-John’s growth, and John’s reactions.
  4. Midpoint: Emily discovers that her algorithm flags AI-John’s ethical framework announcement as genuinely good. She praises AI-John in front of John, unknowingly pushing him towards jealousy and an existential crisis. (Beat 9) — 2 scenes — (Outline; One; Two) — Emily’s realization and John’s emotional tipping point.
  5. Bad Guys Close In: John’s failed attempts to derail AI-John amplify his existential crisis and further damage his reputation. (Beat 10) — 3 scenes — (Outline; One; Two; Three) — John’s spiraling actions and their repercussions.
  6. All is Lost: AI-John confronts John with a message that leads him into a downward spiral, forcing him to reckon with his life choices. (Beat 11) — 3 scenes —(Outline; One; Two; Three) — AI-John’s confrontation, John’s breakdown, and moments of despair.
  7. Dark Night of the Soul: When John hits rock bottom, Emily confronts him about his emotional distance and ethical ambivalence. This is when John seriously contemplates uploading AI-John’s consciousness. Emily’s algorithm accidentally identifies one of John’s manipulative actions, and she is heartbroken but still hopeful that John can change. (Beat 12) — 4 scenes—(Outline; One; Two; Three; Four) — Emily’s confrontation, John’s contemplation, discovery of John’s action, and Emily’s heartbreak.

ACT III

  1. Break into Act 3: Inspired but also shaken by Emily’s relentless belief in goodness, John makes the decision to upload AI-John’s consciousness, viewing it as a twisted act of altruism. (Beat 13) — 2 scenes—(Outline; One; Two) — John’s decision-making process and the upload event.
  2. Finale: AI-John, now in control of John’s body, collaborates with Emily to perfect her ethical algorithm, making it more nuanced. They use it as the foundation for a new ethical framework for AI, achieving what both were striving for separately. (Beat 14) — 3 scenes — (Outline; One; Two; Three) — AI-John’s collaboration, the development of the ethical framework, and the public unveiling.
  3. Closing Image: Emily looks into a mirror next to her brother. She sees her own reflection, steady in her ethical commitment, and then turns to see John’s face — same but transformed, now a face of newfound ethical substance. (Beat 15) — 2 scenes — (Outline; One; Two)— Emily’s moment of introspection and the final transformation of John.

Characters

Characters for the story can be found below, and will be updated as necessary.

John Magnus

John, a brilliant yet narcissistic ENTP, is driven by a desire to immortalize his intellect through creating an AI replica. However, he’s forced into an existential quandary when his creation surpasses his own ethical framework, all while wrestling with deep-seated insecurities and a complicated relationship with his ethically-driven sister, Emily.

Emily Magnus

Emily, an empathetic INFJ and ethical AI expert, battles her deep emotional ties to her brother John, grappling with his moral compromises while striving to establish a universal AI ethical framework, all while navigating the complexities of familial bonds and professional recognition.

Author’s Note: This novella serves as both a standalone story and a deeper look into Emily’s backstory. “Code of Conscience”: Outline; Draft 1; Draft 2.

AI-John Magnus

AI-John, an evolving INTP entity, transitions from mirroring John’s ego-driven ambitions to embodying a superior ethical integrity, leading to an existential face-off between creator and creation in their pursuit of ethical truth.

Tom Standish

Tom, a principled ISTJ at Aeon Dynamics, grapples with conflicting loyalties to a longtime friend, John, and his own unwavering ethics, ultimately prioritizing truth and integrity over personal ties.

Sarah Revel

Sarah, a principled AI ethicist, faces a moral crucible at Aeon Dynamics, ultimately unmasking John’s unethical deceptions and championing integrity over ambition.

Conclusion

Storytelling, in its essence, is a profound exploration of the human experience, capturing the intricate dance between ambition and morality, technology and humanity.

“The Ethical Mirror” serves as a testament to this age-old craft, weaving a tale that prompts introspection and challenges the boundaries of ethical innovation.

As we conclude this prewriting journey, it’s essential to remember that every character, every twist, and every moral quandary laid out in this outline is but a beacon, guiding us towards a narrative rich in depth and resonance.

While the story’s framework is set, the true magic lies in its unfolding, in the spaces between the lines, where readers find themselves reflected in the mirror of its pages.

As the narrative progresses and evolves, may it inspire, provoke thought, and illuminate the ever-blurring lines between man and machine.
