Decoding the Gray Area: Understanding the Fine Line Between Narcissism and Confidence

Exploring the different forms of Narcissism, its impact on relationships, and the importance of distinguishing it from confidence

Narcissism is a personality trait that is often associated with confidence and self-assurance, but it’s essential to understand the difference between the two. Narcissism refers to a set of individual behaviors that often correlate with a serious personality disorder which can have detrimental effects on personal relationships and mental health.
