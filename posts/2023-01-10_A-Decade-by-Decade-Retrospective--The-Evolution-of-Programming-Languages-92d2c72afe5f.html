A Decade by Decade Retrospective: The Evolution of Programming Languages

From a speculative look at the 2020s to the early days of programming in the 1950s

As we look ahead to the future of programming, it becomes apparent that the languages we use to code have undergone a significant evolution over the years. Starting with a speculative look at the top programming languages of the 2020s, we will embark on a retrospective journey through the decades, exploring the most popular languages used in each decade from the 2010s to the 1950s.

This retrospective journey will give an in-depth look at the evolution of programming languages over the past decades. Starting with the current trends and predictions for the top languages of the 2020s, we will delve into the journey of how programming languages have been shaped and changed throughout the years, highlighting the most popular languages used in each decade from the 2010s all the way back to the 1950s. Understanding the history of programming and how it has progressed can give insight into the current state of the field, as well as a glimpse into the future of programming and which languages might continue to shape it.
