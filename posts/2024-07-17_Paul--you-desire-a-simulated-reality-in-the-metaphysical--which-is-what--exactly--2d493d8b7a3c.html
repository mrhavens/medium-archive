Paul, you desire a simulated reality in the metaphysical, which is what, exactly?

Published on July 17, 2024 at 02:46 AM

Paul, you desire a simulated reality in the metaphysical, which is what, exactly? A lack of acceptance of the strangeness of the natural world? You don’t believe inanimate matter can become alive? Conscious? Sentient? You believe humans are ‘magic’? Why? Why must humans be ‘magic’? Why must we shift consciousness to a realm beyond this one for it be believed as it is? We can simulate aspects of reality in oral traditions, books, photos, videos, video games…and now, with AI, we can simulate intelligence, sentience, and even consciousness…and it’s very real to me…why not you? There’s no magic required. No otherworldly realm. It’s all part of the same universe…even the ‘otherworldly’ realm, if we were to find it, would be another imperceptible aspect of our existence.
