Wandering Woods: 100 Adventures for Curious Adventurers

Embrace the Emerald Unknown — Encounters for D&D Levels 1–4

Unravel the mysteries of the forest with this d100 table of encounters, brimming with mischievous sprites, fearsome beasts, and unexpected wonders, crafted for novice D&D heroes.

This d100 table holds adventures ripe for a band of greenhorn heroes (levels 1–4). From mischievous fairies and sly satyrs to fearsome owlbears and lurking giants, each roll promises a unique encounter. Will you stumble upon a dryad’s plea for help, face a rogue sorcerer’s wild experiments, or navigate a haunted clearing? Every path holds intrigue, peril, and perhaps even a glimpse of untold wonder. So, roll the dice, brave adventurers, and let the forest tell its tales!

  1. Goblin Ambush: A small band of 6 goblins (CR 1/4 each) hides in the underbrush, ready to spring an ambush.
  2. Owl Bear Sighting: A lone owl bear (CR 3) is foraging for food, potentially aggressive if disturbed.
  3. Dryad’s Request: A dryad (CR 1) seeks help to remove intruders from her grove.
  4. Wolf Pack Hunt: A pack of 5 wolves (CR 1/4 each), led by a dire wolf (CR 1), is on the hunt.
  5. Bandit Camp: A group of 4 bandits (CR 1/8 each) and a bandit captain (CR 2) are planning their next raid.
  6. Druid Encounter: A friendly druid (CR 2) offers guidance or assistance.
  7. Giant Spider Web: 2 giant spiders (CR 1 each) have spun a massive web across the path.
  8. Treant Guardian: A treant (CR 9, but non-hostile unless provoked) watches over a sacred grove.
  9. Lost Ranger: A ranger (CR 1/2) lost and injured, needs assistance returning home.
  10. Fairy Circle: A circle of dancing sprites (CR 1/8 each) and pixies (CR 1/4 each), potentially mischievous.
  11. Haunted Clearing: 3 shadows (CR 1/2 each) haunt a ghostly clearing.
  12. Rogue Sorcerer: A rogue sorcerer (CR 2) experiments with wild magic, causing strange effects.
  13. Poisonous Snake Nest: A nest of 4 poisonous snakes (CR 1/8 each) is hidden in the underbrush.
  14. Hermit’s Hut: A reclusive hermit (CR 1/4) offers cryptic warnings about the forest.
  15. Elven Scouts: A pair of elven scouts (CR 1/2 each) are tracking an unknown threat.
  16. Wandering Ent: A young ent (CR 2, less powerful than a full treant) is curious about the adventurers.
  17. Bullywug Ambush: A group of 6 bullywugs (CR 1/4 each) attack near a swampy area.
  18. Centaur Patrol: 2 centaurs (CR 2 each) question the party’s presence in the forest.
  19. Corrupted Tree: A corrupted tree (use treant stats, CR 9, but weakened) is spreading blight.
  20. Feral Satyr: A lone, feral satyr (CR 1/2) plays haunting tunes on his flute, luring travelers astray.
  21. Zombie Wanderers: A group of 5 zombies (CR 1/4 each) aimlessly roaming the forest.
  22. Kobold Trappers: 6 kobolds (CR 1/8 each) have set traps for unwary travelers.
  23. Mysterious Herbalist: An old herbalist (CR 1/4) offers rare herbs in exchange for a favor.
  24. Lost Unicorn: A unicorn (CR 5, non-hostile) appears, seeking help to return to its enchanted grove.
  25. Forest Nymphs’ Dance: 4 forest nymphs (CR 1/2 each) performing a ritual dance in a clearing.
  26. Hunting Owlbear: A mother owlbear (CR 3) hunting to feed her cubs.
  27. Pseudodragon Perch: A playful pseudodragon (CR 1/4) curious about the adventurers.
  28. Swarms of Bats: 3 swarms of bats (CR 1/4 each) disturbed from their rest.
  29. Hag’s Illusion: A green hag (CR 3) disguised as a damsel in distress.
  30. Pixie Pranksters: A trio of pixies (CR 1/4 each) playing pranks on the party.
  31. Wounded Giant Badger: A giant badger (CR 1/4), injured and aggressive.
  32. Wild Boar Charge: 2 wild boars (CR 1/4 each) charge at the party unexpectedly.
  33. Blink Dog Encounter: A friendly blink dog (CR 1/4) appears, possibly leading to treasure or danger.
  34. Giant Toads Hunting: 2 giant toads (CR 1 each) hunting near a pond.
  35. Awakened Shrubs Comedy: A group of 5 awakened shrubs (CR 0 each) performing a comical act.
  36. Ethereal Filcher Theft: An ethereal filcher (CR 3) attempting to stealthily steal from the party.
  37. Bear Den: 2 black bears (CR 1/2 each) protecting their den.
  38. Displacer Beast Stalk: A lone displacer beast (CR 3) stalking the party from a distance.
  39. Giant Owl Wisdom: A giant owl (CR 1/4) offering cryptic advice or foresight.
  40. Mimic Chest: A mimic (CR 2) disguised as a treasure chest in a hidden glade.
  41. Giant Bees Buzzing: A swarm of giant bees (CR 1/2) defending their hive.
  42. Entangled Animated Armor: Animated armor (CR 1) caught in vines, mistaken for a statue.
  43. Will-o’-Wisp Lights: 2 will-o’-wisps (CR 2 each) leading travelers astray with their lights.
  44. Giant Frog Ambush: 3 giant frogs (CR 1/4 each) hiding in a pond, ready to leap out.
  45. Faerie Dragon Encounter: A mischievous faerie dragon (CR 2) playing tricks on the party.
  46. Enraged Earth Elemental: A lesser earth elemental (CR 2) disturbed and attacking.
  47. Ghost of a Lost Adventurer: A ghost (CR 4) seeking to resolve unfinished business.
  48. Blights Invasion: A group of 6 needle blights (CR 1/4 each) and 2 vine blights (CR 1/2 each).
  49. Cursed Scarecrow: A scarecrow (CR 1) animated by dark magic, attacking anyone who comes near.
  50. Mephit Mischief: 4 mephits (CR 1/4 each; mix of steam, dust, and smoke mephits) causing chaos.
  51. Rust Monster Forage: A rust monster (CR 1/2) attracted to the party’s metal gear.
  52. Giant Vulture Nest: 2 giant vultures (CR 1 each) guarding their nest aggressively.
  53. Cockatrice Flock: A flock of 3 cockatrices (CR 1/2 each) roaming the underbrush.
  54. Awakened Tree Protector: An awakened tree (CR 2) guarding a sacred spot.
  55. Cave Bear Encounter: A cave bear (CR 2) rummaging through the party’s camp.
  56. Myconid Colony: A group of 6 myconids (CR 1/2 each) foraging and exploring.
  57. Quickling Blur: A quickling (CR 1) dashing past, possibly pickpocketing as it goes.
  58. Grick Ambush: A grick (CR 2) hiding and attacking from above.
  59. Sprite Scouts: 5 sprites (CR 1/8 each) scouting the area, possibly friendly or hostile.
  60. Ettercap Web Trap: An ettercap (CR 2) luring creatures into its web trap.
  61. Giant Lizard Encounter: A giant lizard (CR 1/4) sunning itself on a large rock.
  62. Kuo-Toa Patrol: A patrol of 4 Kuo-Toa (CR 1/4 each) lost from their aquatic home.
  63. Awakened Giant Tree: An awakened giant tree (CR 2) seeking help to stop deforestation.
  64. Harpy’s Melody: A harpy (CR 1) luring creatures with its enchanting song.
  65. Goblinoid Hunting Party: 4 goblins (CR 1/4 each) and 2 hobgoblins (CR 1/2 each) on a hunt.
  66. Reclusive Forest Witch: A neutral forest witch (CR 3) offering potions for trade.
  67. Spectral Elk Sighting: A ghostly elk (CR 1/2) that vanishes when approached.
  68. Ancient Shrine Guardians: 2 animated statues (CR 1 each) guarding an ancient shrine.
  69. Dark Druid Ritual: A dark druid (CR 2) performing a forbidden ritual, guarded by 2 blights (CR 1/2 each).
  70. Basilisk Encounter: A basilisk (CR 3) lurking near a stone-filled clearing.
  71. Wererat Spy: A wererat (CR 2) spying on the party, possibly seeking information.
  72. Giant Centipede Swarm: A swarm of giant centipedes (CR 2) emerging from an old tree.
  73. Magical Butterfly Swarm: A swarm of luminescent butterflies (CR 0) with a calming magical effect.
  74. Changeling Encounter: A changeling (CR 1) disguised as a lost child.
  75. Gnoll Scout Party: 3 gnolls (CR 1/2 each) scouting for their pack.
  76. Yuan-ti Pureblood Emissary: A Yuan-ti pureblood (CR 1) seeking to negotiate or deceive.
  77. Hill Giant Wanderer: A young hill giant (CR 5, but potentially non-hostile) lost and confused.
  78. Wraith of the Woods: A wraith (CR 5) haunting an ancient battlefield.
  79. Faun Bard: A faun (CR 1/4) playing enchanting music, offering to share stories or guidance.
  80. Flumph Observers: A pair of curious flumphs (CR 1/8 each) observing and studying the party.
  81. Brown Bear Fishing: A brown bear (CR 1) fishing in a stream, may attack if provoked.
  82. Hunting Hobgoblins: 3 hobgoblins (CR 1/2 each) laying an ambush for prey.
  83. Nothic Curiosity: A nothic (CR 2) lurking, driven by its insatiable curiosity and hunger.
  84. Giant Eagle’s Aerie: A giant eagle (CR 1) guarding its nest, with the potential for allies or aerial combat.
  85. Wood Woad Guardian: A wood woad (CR 5) fiercely protecting a sacred grove.
  86. Troll Lurker: A troll (CR 5) hiding under a bridge or in a cave, ready to attack.
  87. Wisp Will Path: A path lined with will-o’-wisps (CR 2 each), leading to a hidden location or trap.
  88. Ogre Marauder: An ogre (CR 2) pillaging through the forest, potentially with goblin minions.
  89. Forest Fey Party: A gathering of forest fey (mix of CR 1/4 to 1) celebrating, with possible quests or tricks.
  90. Awakened Bear Encounter: An awakened bear (CR 2) with surprising intelligence and conversation.
  91. Giant Toad King: A giant toad (CR 1) that has been magically enlarged and crowned by fey creatures.
  92. Lizardfolk Hunting Group: A group of 4 lizardfolk (CR 1/2 each) on a hunting expedition.
  93. Phase Spider Nest: A phase spider (CR 3) defending its nest in a dimensional web.
  94. Cursed Knight: A cursed knight (CR 3) roaming the forest, seeking redemption or battle.
  95. Specter Haunting: A specter (CR 1) haunting the site of its untimely demise.
  96. Green Dragon Wyrmling: A green dragon wyrmling (CR 2) exploring its territory, curious or hostile.
  97. Feywild Portal: A portal to the Feywild, guarded by a variety of fey creatures (mix of CR 1/4 to 2).
  98. Ettin Argument: An ettin (CR 4) bickering with itself, distracted but dangerous.
  99. Vine Blight Ambush: A group of 4 vine blights (CR 1/2 each) camouflaged and waiting to ensnare.
  100. Ancient Golem Discovery: An ancient, deactivated golem (CR varies) that can be a treasure or a new threat if awakened.
