Understanding and Overcoming Victim Blaming

Examining the impact and strategies for ending the blame game

Imagine being gaslighted, manipulated, and blamed for the abuse inflicted upon you by a narcissistic partner.

Victim blaming is a common tactic used by narcissists to deflect responsibility for their actions, and it’s time to break free from that cycle.

In this article, we will explore the specific ways in which narcissistic abuse can lead to victim blaming and reveal the strategies for recognizing, challenging, and overcoming it.
