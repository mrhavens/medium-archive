The “Dude with a Problem” Logline Template: Because Even Heroes Need a Little Help Sometimes

Published on January 05, 2023 at 08:54 AM

25 “Dude With a Problem” Logline Templates: Because Even Heroes Need a Little Help Sometimes

Don’t let your hero’s crisis go unnoticed — use these templates to craft a compelling logline

Attention fellow storytellers!

Are you struggling to come up with the perfect logline for your “Dude with a Problem” tale?

Yes?

Well, fear not because I’ve got you covered with my list of tried and true logline templates.

You know the drill — a hero finds themselves in a pile of shit, facing a crisis that they have to overcome.

Films like “Die Hard” and “The Hunger Games” have made the “Dude with a Problem” genre a fan favorite for a reason.

Now, it’s your turn to add your own twist.

These logline templates are here to help you craft the perfect pitch for your crisis-conquering tale.

Give them a shot and watch as your logline transforms from ‘eh’ to ‘oh yeah!’ in no time.

Here are twenty-five logline templates for “Dude with a Problem” stories:

  1. “When a [regular guy/everyman/unlikely hero] is [thrown/forced/tasked] into a [deadly situation/dangerous mission/mysterious puzzle], they must [use their skills/wits/expertise] to [survive/solve the mystery/uncover the truth] and [save the day/stop the threat/prevent disaster].”
