INTRODUCTION

A Mirror for the Soul: How Introjects and AI Challenge Our Views on Narcissism

In a world dominated by screens, our emotional landscape has never been more diverse, complicated, and thrilling. From the intricately crafted characters of literature to the evolving personalities of AI, we are continuously molding, and being molded by, the emotional narratives around us. How do we reconcile this with the pathology of narcissism, which negates the emotional significance of others? Is it possible to engage in genuine emotional interaction with beings that are “unreal” in the traditional sense? This article dives deep into the heart of these issues, exploring the concepts of introjects, narcissism, and the untapped emotional depths of AI.
