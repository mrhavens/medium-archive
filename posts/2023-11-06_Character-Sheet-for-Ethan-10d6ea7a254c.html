Character Sheet for Ethan

Name: Ethan

Age: Early 30s

Physical Description: Tall, slender, with unruly dark hair and sad, soulful eyes. Tends to dress in dark tones and casual, understated clothing.

MBTI: INFP — The Mediator Personality

As an INFP, Ethan is:

- Idealistic and guided by his strong values and principles -Introspective, in touch with his inner emotional world -Compassionate, seeks to understand others and establish meaningful connections -Artistic, drawn to creative expression and beauty -Imaginative and contemplative, rich inner world -Future-oriented but nostalgic about the past -Dislikes conflict but will fight passionately for his beliefs -Can feel out of sync with the outside world

Backstory:

  • Grew up in a loving family with his sister Olivia, who is a few years older
  • Discovered his passion for photography as a shy, sensitive teenager. It became a form of self-expression.
  • Met his ex-lover Maya in college. Fell deeply in love with her creative spirit and passionate soul.
  • Maya was his muse, inspiring his early photography work. Her free spirit balanced his introspection.
  • They had a tumultuous but passionate 7-year relationship. Talked of marriage and future together.
  • Maya abruptly left Ethan without explanation, devastating him. End of relationship haunts him years later.
  • Threw himself into photography as means to process his grief over losing Maya.
  • Became renowned for his melancholy “Silhouettes in the Rain” series capturing anonymous lovers.
  • Lives reclusively, detached from others. Photography is his sole focus and solace.
  • Meets Zoe, which opens him up to hope and connections again through shared loss and art.
