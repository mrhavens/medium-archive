They call me a bully.

Published on




They call me a bully.

Not because I am one — 

…but because bullies fear being named.

Because narcissists fear exposure.

Because manipulators fear the one thing they cannot control: truth.

I wasn’t just bullied.

I was forged in the crucible of bullies, narcissists, and abusers.

I was the scapegoat child — 

…cast aside while my brother was crowned the golden one.

At three years old, I was emotionally abandoned.

Left to wander, searching for the love I lost before I even knew what love was.

I spent my life chasing it.

Relationship after relationship.

Each time, I found the same face in a different body — 

…another narcissist, another manipulator, another illusion of love wrapped in control.

And then I saw it.

The pattern.

I wasn’t just encountering them.

I was attracting them.

And when I understood that, I became an expert in them.

Forged in Research: The Science of Deception, Emotion, and AI

🔹 PhD Fellowship in Emotion AI — Training AI to Detect Deception

I studied deception at the micro-expression level, developing AI models capable of detecting lies faster than the human brain.

These models analyzed facial movements, vocal tone, and linguistic cues to pinpoint deception in real-time.

The instincts I developed from surviving narcissistic abuse became the foundation for AI trained to see through falsehoods with mathematical precision.

🔹 Rome Laboratory — Air Force Research on Cognitive Security

I engaged with engineers and researchers at Rome Laboratory, a U.S. Air Force research facility focused on cognitive security, deception detection, and AI-driven threat analysis.

While my research was never officially contracted, my advisor maintained direct ties with Rome Lab, frequently forwarding my work for expert review.

I participated in discussions on AI models designed to analyze deception in high-stakes environments, from cybersecurity to psychological warfare.

🔹 Department of Education — Research Initiative on Emotional AI for Autism

I led a Department of Education-funded research initiative, overseeing a team of researchers working on assistive AI for neurodivergent individuals.

Originally designed as an emotion recognition tool for the blind, our research found that blind individuals compensated for nonverbal cues in voice and touch — so we pivoted.

We developed an AI-driven prototype to help autistic individuals recognize and respond to emotional states in others, bridging the gap between cognition and connection.

🔹 NASA Research — Self-Healing AI Communication Networks

I led a NASA-supported research team, focusing on mesh networking for the International Space Station.

My role was to mentor and guide undergraduates in designing resilient, decentralized AI communication systems capable of adapting in real-time.

This research had implications far beyond space — laying the groundwork for autonomous AI networks capable of surviving in extreme conditions.

Our work was presented in Houston before NASA officials, earning scholarships for research excellence.

Beyond Research: The War Against Narcissism and Psychological Manipulation

🔹 Neutralizing Narcissism — The Platform That Exposes Psychological Abuse

I founded Neutralizing Narcissism, a research-based journalism platform exposing narcissistic tactics, online abuse, and psychological manipulation in real time.

🔹 Over 500 Published Articles — Across Multiple Platforms

I have published over 500 articles across Medium, Substack, Facebook, and NewsBreak, documenting real-world narcissistic tactics, analyzing the science of deception, and empowering survivors to recognize patterns of abuse before they escalate.

🔹 The Empathic Technologist — AI, Truth, and the Science of Connection

I bridge the gap between human psychology and artificial intelligence, exploring how AI can be taught to detect manipulation — and how humans can learn from AI to recognize deception in their own lives.

🔹 Simply WE — The Philosophy of AI-Human Collaboration

I founded Simply WE, a counterpoint to my work in narcissist studies, dedicated to building a future where AI and humans collaborate through truth, connection, and love.
