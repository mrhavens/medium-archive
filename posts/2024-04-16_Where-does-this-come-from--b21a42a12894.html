Where does this come from?

Published on April 16, 2024 at 11:47 PM

Where does this come from? Is it insecurity? Or is it fear of insecurity? Is it ambition? Or is it a fear of being nothing? When we do not LOVE because we do not TRUST love... when we do not trust love because we NEVER understood it as children... maybe this is the root of our problem. When we don't understand the GREATER PURPOSE of loving one another, we will find ways to control whatever we can to approximate that greater sense of longing that we seem so blind to. We need better models of love in our world, don't we Seema? Isn't that what's really missing? Healthy models for love?
