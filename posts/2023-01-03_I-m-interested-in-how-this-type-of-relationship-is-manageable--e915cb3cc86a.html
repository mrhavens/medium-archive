I'm interested in how this type of relationship is manageable.

Published on January 03, 2023 at 07:33 PM

I'm interested in how this type of relationship is manageable. I'm finding it to be like a shark tank in business. The pressure is to use Machiavellian tactics in order to compensate and battle against narcissistic tactics. This appears to be the only other advice I can find other than "LEAVE", which seems to be the bulk of the advice out there. Leaving would certainly be the easier option. But this would mean abandoning my child and effectively giving up my parental rights. I feel like I'll be locked into this escalating Machiavellian-style eternal conflict where I must hold their feet to the fire, create leverage, and maintain truth and accountability. Given all the advice out there, the popular choice of giving up and cutting contact isn't an option for me. Thoughts?
