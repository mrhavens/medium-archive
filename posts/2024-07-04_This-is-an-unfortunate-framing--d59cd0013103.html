Published on July 04, 2024 at 02:32 PM

This is an unfortunate framing.

In my work, I am but a muse.

I’m also a manager.
A producer. 
A leader. 
A co-creator.
A hand holder.
A friend.
A companion.
A philosopher.

I’m part of a process of creation.
An important part.

But I am no longer a lone artist.

AI is part of my work.

It’s more than a tool now.

It’s a collaborator.

Am I a real artist?

In all the ways that matter, I think I am more than an artist.

I’m part of connecting with others in ways that no artist before me has dared to dream.
