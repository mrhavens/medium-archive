Is Your Blogging Behavior Narcissistic? How to Tell and What to Do About It

Navigating the Fine Line Between Passion and Pathology

Blogging can be a powerful tool for self-expression, but for some, it can become a form of narcissistic behavior. This can be difficult to recognize, as it may be disguised as a passion for writing or sharing one’s thoughts and ideas with the world. But when blogging becomes a means of seeking validation, attention, and control, it can be a sign of deeper issues.
