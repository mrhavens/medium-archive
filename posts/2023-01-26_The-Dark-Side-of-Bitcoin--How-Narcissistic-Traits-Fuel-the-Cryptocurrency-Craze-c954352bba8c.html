The Dark Side of Bitcoin: How Narcissistic Traits Fuel the Cryptocurrency Craze

Exploring the intersection of personality disorders and decentralized finance

Crypto-narcissist — A term used to describe individuals who exhibit narcissistic traits and are heavily involved in the cryptocurrency market. These individuals may be driven by a desire for power and control, as well as a belief in their own superior understanding of the technology.

Have you ever wondered why some people are so passionate about Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies? It may be more than just the potential for financial gain — a new study suggests that there may be a link between narcissistic personality traits and involvement in the decentralized finance space.
