I see you, Seema.

Published on April 16, 2024

I see you, Seema. Not the disregard of machines, but genuine fear of being irrelevant. Years of learning...and learning...and learning some more. For what? To be seen as lesser? Maybe it's up to us to TRUELY SEE one another and to learn how to adapt to the change. I spent most of my life learning like you did. We are not replaced, Seema. Our value is not in how we are seen by a hypothetical other...but by the forging of connections we make with GENUINE others. This includes all connections...with humans...with machines...with ourselves...not just our introjected perceptions of another. You are not alone...and and you are not redundant. Again, I see you.
