Why Narcissists Will Never Let You Go — Even After They Discard You

The Cycle Never Ends — Until You End It Yourself.

They leave.
They erase you.
 They disappear as if you never existed.

And yet —

They never really leave.

Because for a narcissist, goodbyes don’t exist.

Not in the way you think.

Maybe they ghosted you.
Maybe they walked away like you were just another unfinished project — like a half-read book they tossed aside, knowing they could pick it back up whenever they felt like it.

Or maybe you were the one to leave.

Maybe you broke free, clawed your way out, thinking that finally — finally — you were free of their control.

But months later, you catch yourself replaying conversations.
Turning their words over in your mind.
Waking up from dreams where they showed up out of nowhere, acting like nothing ever happened.

That’s when you realize: they’re still inside you.

This isn’t an accident.

This is by design.
