INTRODUCTION

Have you ever felt like an emotional detective, piecing together fragments of a relationship, hoping for a picture that finally makes sense?

I have.

It’s as if you’re walking through a dark forest, guided only by the flickering light of your own intuition.

You sense something is missing, but you can’t quite put your finger on it.

What you’re grappling with is an emotional void, an unsettling emptiness that lurks in the corners of your relationships. And you’re not alone; I’ve been there, too.

This article is a compass, aimed to navigate you through the misty terrain of emotional unavailability.

We’ll uncover its roots, often intertwined with narcissism, explore its toll on empathic and codependent individuals, and finally, present strategies to reclaim your emotional space.

Because, in the end, your emotional well-being isn’t just a concept; it’s a right.
