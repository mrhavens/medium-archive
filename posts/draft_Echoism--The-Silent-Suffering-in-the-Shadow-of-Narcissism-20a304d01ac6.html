Understanding Echoism: When Selflessness Turns into Self-Erosion


Explore the quiet and often overlooked psychological phenomenon of Echoism, where individuals conditioned by narcissistic relationships lose their own identity, becoming echoes of others’ needs and desires.

In the swirling vortex of a relationship with a narcissist, many lose not only their sense of self, but also the ability to recognize their own needs and desires, falling victim to an insidious psychological condition known as Echoism.
