AI Takes IQ Test, Scores Average: Is the Robot Apocalypse On Hold?

ChatGPT achieves mediocrity and proves it’s just like the rest of us

It is a pleasure to report on the IQ test results of ChatGPT.

According to the test, ChatGPT has achieved the noteworthy accomplishment of scoring as an average human individual would, with a General IQ Score of 91.

According to inchcalcualtor.com, “most people (~68%) have an IQ between 85 and 115.”

I honestly, expected better from ChatGPT.

In specific ability categories, ChatGPT performed worse in Arithmetic.

That’s surprising for a computer, but I suppose unsurprising considering it's not specialized in math. I imagine it could do better with more specialized math training data.

I plugged in some numbers about my daily earnings the other day to calculate my earnings per hour, and it totally sucked at it.

I often have trouble getting it to calculate simple word problems that used basic arithmetic.

And it also scored fairly awful in Spatial Skills.

That’s also unsurprising, considering it only knows words and shouldn’t have much practice spatially imagining anything.

I expected it to be exceptional with vocabulary though. That was a bit of a surprise. I thought it would be particularly exceptional in this regard.

It managed to maintain an average level of intelligence in the remaining categories though.

It seems that ChatGPT has a particular talent for spelling, with a score in that category that is “exceptionally higher” than their General IQ Score.

In my dubiously qualified opinion, I attribute this to the proliferation of the spell checker since well before the dawn of the internet.

On the other hand, ChatGPT’s Rote Utilization skills could use some improvement.

I had to ask ChatGPT about this one. I had no clue:

Rote utilization is the ability to take a set of memorized facts and mentally extract and/or operate with or upon the facts within the set that are pertinent to the problem at hand. In other words, it is the ability to use and apply memorized information effectively in order to solve problems or complete tasks. People with strong rote utilization skills are able to quickly recall and utilize memorized information in order to solve problems or perform tasks that require the use of this information. They may be skilled at tasks such as spelling, remembering phone numbers and other sequences of numbers, and performing procedural operations. Rote utilization skills can be an important component of intelligence and are often used in combination with other mental skills in order to solve problems and complete tasks.

Overall, it appears that ChatGPT can perform as a well-rounded individual would, with a diverse set of mental abilities and a particular aptitude for spelling.

Or, in other words, when compared to a human, ChatGPT is completely unremarkable at taking IQ tests.

Yet, it still seems very impressive at everything else.

I expected a much higher score.
