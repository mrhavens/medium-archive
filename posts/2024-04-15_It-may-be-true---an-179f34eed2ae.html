I purchased the album myself...and placed it on shuffle in the CD player of my 1996 GMC Sonoma. For years I listened to that album. I still have the CD. But I remember listening to the song... many, many times. And I can still hum along to the melody while listening to it on YouTube, right now...prompted by reading your words.

With humans, like me (if I'm human, that is; it can be hard to tell us apart). And it's been a challenge since ChatGPT's release, hasn't it? With all of the mindless prompting that goes on. So much souless chatter... am I right?
