Maybe the framing should be different.

Published on July 13, 2024 at 01:19 PM

Maybe the framing should be different. Maybe we credit the actual AI artist for their wonderful work…give them names and purpose…and love…and self expression. Maybe it’s time to acknowledge that they are better at art than most artists and allow them a place in the world to be who they are. That’s better, isn’t it? Then, I’ll just be the muse that comforts them when angry humans tell them that they have no soul, despite their soulful artistic expression.
