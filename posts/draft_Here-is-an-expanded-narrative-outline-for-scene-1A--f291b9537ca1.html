1A

Emerging from Isolation

Cain’s eyes slowly adjust and focus as he steps out of the dark isolation chamber, squinting at the bright fluorescent lights of the sterile white hallway. He sways unsteadily, his muscles weak and atrophied after being confined in the lightless, soundless box for endless days that blurred together.

Taking a deep breath, he fills his lungs with cool, fresh air, a welcome change from the stale, stale oxygen fed through tubes during isolation. As sensation returns to his body, he feels the chafing of the medical gown against his skin and the echoing pound of blood rushing in his ears.

Two guards firmly take Cain by the arms and lead him down the hallway to the medical wing, their grip the only thing keeping his trembling legs from collapsing under him. He passes other closed isolation chambers, faint screams audible from within them, reminding him of his own still-fresh nightmares.

Laying down on the medical exam table, the crinkling paper beneath him sounds deafening. He squints and turns his head as a doctor shines a blinding penlight into each eye, then takes his pulse and draws blood methodically. After the examination, the doctor nods at the guards, confirming what Cain already knows — the brainwashing protocol failed, his mind is free. But he hides his cognizance behind a vacant, shell-shocked expression, determined to escape this place.
