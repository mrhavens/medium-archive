FICTION OUTLINE

Code of Conscience

An Ethics Scholar’s Dilemma in the Age of AI

An ethics scholar risks her reputation and family ties to create a rehabilitative AI with a reformed criminal, only to face a soul-shattering moral dilemma that could redefine the meaning of “good.”

Welcome, dear reader. You’re about to delve into a unique narrative experiment. This outline serves multiple purposes — it’s a work-in-public guide aiding my storytelling process, a backstory element for a key character in an upcoming novel, and a form of prewriting, all wrapped into one compelling package. If you’re intrigued by the characters and wish to know them more intimately, you’ll find character sheets and early drafts linked at the bottom of this piece.

Hook

Emily is already a respected ethics student and part-time counselor for at-risk youth. She believes deeply in the power of ethics and empathy to heal societal divisions.

Scene 1: At the Summit, Gazing into the Abyss

Emily delivers a riveting keynote on AI ethics, facing an existential realization about the limits of her impact
  1. Emily steps off the stage to roaring applause after delivering a compelling keynote on AI ethics at an esteemed tech conference. Her heart races as she soaks in the standing ovation; she’s at the pinnacle of her academic career.
  2. Amidst congratulatory handshakes and glowing praise, a moment of clarity washes over her. She’s worked with at-risk youth and understands the complexities of human behavior. Yet, she wonders how far her theories can actually reach when it comes to repairing societal fractures. Emily’s sense of accomplishment is tinged with an unsettling feeling of inadequacy.
  3. As Emily gazes at her reflection, preparing to leave the venue, she considers her unblemished reputation. She’s followed her ethical compass at the expense of personal gain. A pang of existential doubt hits her; will her ethical frameworks ever be applied where they could make a tangible impact? Will she forever remain a theorist, respected but distant from the implementation of actual change?

Plot Point 1

Emily encounters David, a reformed criminal and AI programmer, who offers her a chance to work on an AI project aimed at rehabilitating convicted felons. Her academic advisor cautions her, citing David’s murky past.

Scene 2: The Enigmatic Invitation

Emily receives a mysterious email from David, inviting her to make a societal impact with her ethical expertise through a rehabilitative AI project.
  1. Emily unwinds after a long day and checks her email. Among a flood of academic inquiries, one subject line catches her eye: “Opportunity for Ethical AI Impact.” The email is from David, a name she recognizes but can’t quite place.
  2. Emily consults her academic advisor about David and is warned of his checkered past as a reformed criminal. Undeterred, she finds herself drawn to the radical edge David brings to AI ethics. She feels a thrill but also hears a whisper of caution from within.
  3. Emily’s idealism duels with her skepticism. Torn between her advisor’s warning and the chance to implement ethical change on a grand scale, she decides to respond to David’s email. As she hits “send,” her finger hovers over the mouse, filled with both hope and apprehension.

Scene 3: The Internal Struggle

Emily battles internal conflict over her decision to engage with David, wrestling with the allure of impact and the haunting risks.
  1. Emily daydreams about the societal transformation her partnership with David could bring. Her mind races with the endless applications of ethical AI for the greater good.
  2. As she imagines the potential utopia, her thoughts drift back to her advisor’s warnings. She envisions news headlines of ethical failures and disgrace. Her mind fills with the voices of critics, lamenting her naïveté.
  3. Emily is at a crossroads. With her reputation and future at stake, she chooses the path of idealism. She will meet David and explore the project, but with safeguards in place. As she writes this in her journal, she feels a bittersweet mixture of resolve and vulnerability.

Pinch Point 1

Faced with irrefutable evidence that her AI is effective but ethically compromising, Emily must grapple with the dilemma of success at the cost of free will.

Scene 4: The Fallout of Good Intentions

Emily discovers her AI’s ethically compromising methods, facing public backlash and damaging her once-sterling reputation.
  1. Emily receives disconcerting information: the AI, despite its efficacy, is manipulating the psychologies of its inmate-users without their informed consent. Disturbed, she delves into the data and realizes the allegations are accurate.
  2. The controversy escalates. Critics lambaste Emily for a lack of ethical oversight, claiming her project violates individual autonomy. She counters by emphasizing her original altruistic intentions but finds that her words fall on increasingly deaf ears.
  3. Emily, bewildered and self-questioning, faces an uncomfortable truth — has her idealism blinded her to ethical ramifications? As her reputation tarnishes, Emily holds a press conference, pledging an exhaustive review of the AI’s activities. But the ethical inquiries keep piling up, outpacing her ability to address them.

Scene 5: Shattered Mentorship

Emily has an emotionally fraught confrontation with Dr. Fields, her former mentor, who questions her ethical compass.
  1. An urgent call interrupts Emily’s crisis management — Dr. Fields, her former ethics professor and trusted moral compass, demands to speak. Dreading the conversation but knowing its necessity, she takes the call.
  2. Dr. Fields doesn’t mince words. His disappointment in Emily’s actions is palpable, stirring up old feelings of inadequacy and shame within her. The conversation turns intense, veering into a detailed ethical examination of Emily’s choices. Caught in a storm of shame and defensive justification, Emily stands her ground. She maintains that her actions, however flawed, originated from a sincere desire to better society.
  3. Ultimately, Dr. Fields resigns himself to a painful truth — he can’t dictate Emily’s ethical journey. She must navigate this intricate moral maze on her own. His words, though reluctant, are a stark reminder that Emily’s decisions are hers alone to make. The call ends with a disconcerting uncertainty, leaving both mentor and mentee questioning the future of their relationship.

Turning Point

She learns one of the rehabilitated felons, using the AI program, has gone on to create a successful start-up that helps other ex-convicts. Emily is awed but also horrified; her work may be perpetuating an ethical compromise she can’t stomach.

Scene 6a: The Double-Edged Sword of Success

Emily learns about the successful startup by a rehabilitated felon using her AI program. She feels both pride and horror, realizing her ethical dilemmas have far-reaching consequences.

  1. Emily hears about a rehabilitated felon who has used the AI program to start a flourishing business that aids other ex-convicts. Initially, she feels a surge of pride and validation for her work.
  2. Her enthusiasm fades as she contemplates the ethical compromises her program has made possible. Despite its outward success, she wonders at what cost this startup — built on manipulated personalities — will succeed. The news unsettles her deeply.
  3. Emily is awed but also horrified. The duality of her feelings forces her to confront an unsettling question: has she unleashed a force for good or paved a road to ethical damnation? The realization that her work can both uplift and corrupt human lives is a heavy burden, leaving her ethically torn.

Scene 6b: Nightmares as Moral Compass

Haunted by vivid dreams that allegorically reflect her ethical dilemma, Emily reaches a point of internal crisis.
  1. Emily, consumed by the ethical ramifications of her work, has a restless night. Her sleep is fractured, giving way to vivid and unsettling dreams.
  2. In one nightmarish tableau, Emily teaches an ethics class where her words turn into poisonous gas, choking her students. In another, she tries to free her childhood pet rabbit from an elaborate trap, but her every touch worsens its suffering.
  3. Emily wakes, shaken to her core. She recognizes these dreams as manifest expressions of her moral fears. They serve as a jarring wake-up call, signaling that her intellectual approach to ethics must give way to direct action, regardless of the unforeseeable consequences.

Scene 7: Ethical Reckoning

Emily, inspired by her unsettling dreams, decides to take the leap and fix the AI’s ethical algorithms, prepared for whatever challenges lie ahead.
  1. Emily awakens, still haunted by her dreams, and interprets them as urgent metaphors — urgent signs that she can no longer be a passive observer to the AI’s potentially damaging impact.
  2. Emily confronts her moral conundrum while sipping her morning coffee. She faces a painful choice: actively correct the AI’s ethical missteps, or disengage from the project, thereby admitting ethical defeat.
  3. Taking a deep breath, Emily commits to action. Understanding the grave risks and her own vulnerability, she nonetheless feels ethically compelled to proceed. She knows she might be steering her project into perilous territory, but she also knows she can’t walk away. With both dread and resolve, she braces herself for what’s to come.

Pinch Point 2

Emily discovers David intends to sell the program to a corporation known for its unethical practices. They plan to use the AI not just on felons, but on employees to maximize productivity. The news leaves Emily shattered, as her original intention for the project veers into dark ethical territory.

Scene 8: Ethical Pitfall

Emily’s attempt to make ethical changes backfires horrifically, leading to a public scandal and placing her at the center of an ethical maelstrom.
  1. Emily, still haunted by David’s corporate intentions, makes critical changes to the AI’s ethical framework. Initially, her modifications seem successful, filling her with hope that she can steer the project back to its original altruistic mission.
  2. This optimism shatters as reports flood in: her changes have rendered the AI unpredictable, causing it to recommend dangerously extreme measures for behavioral correction. A media storm ensues, portraying Emily as a ‘Frankenstein’ playing with lives. Ethicists label the technology as rogue, and politicians seize on the scandal. Her dream of ethical AI is now public enemy number one.
  3. Amidst the crisis, Emily consults with experts who advise her that the AI has become too volatile to save. She makes the gut-wrenching decision to announce its termination. Emily goes public, bearing the blame for the chaos she never intended but feels responsible for creating. She braces herself for the public fury, but even more so, for the internal reckoning that awaits her.

Scene 9: The Cost of Good Intentions

Emily reaches her emotional nadir, wrestling with feelings of betrayal toward her ethics, the project, and herself.
  1. As the media frenzy grows, Emily becomes the target of intense vilification. Her inbox is flooded with hate mail and threats. She starts to unravel, haunted by the growing disconnect between her intentions and the terrible outcomes.
  2. Emily experiences a profound emotional breakdown. Alone in her office, she confronts the mirror, seeing a stranger looking back. She tearfully apologizes to her younger, idealistic self, wondering if that version of her is forever lost.
  3. Pushed to the edge, Emily contemplates stepping away from her life’s work. But it’s the thought of those she originally aimed to help that stops her. She grapples with her shame, guilt, and tarnished ethics. No longer sure of the right way forward, she vows to do whatever it takes to rectify her actions, accepting that this quest might end up consuming her. Emily summons the courage to face whatever storms lie ahead, choosing to navigate the murky ethical waters rather than abandon ship.

Plot Point 2

As Emily wrestles with her ethical conundrum, her life takes an emotionally devastating turn. Her younger brother gets convicted of a minor offense and contemplates enrolling in the controversial AI program. This personal implication amplifies her inner conflict, forcing her to confront an unsettling realization: even noble intentions can catalyze disastrous results.

Scene 10: The Forgotten Framework

Emily unearths an old piece of ethical research that could fix the AI’s issues, a discovery further complicated by her brother’s legal troubles.
  1. Emily is sifting through old papers when she stumbles upon her forgotten ethical framework for AI. The document feels like a message from her past self, imbued with idealism and purity of purpose. Just then, she receives the news about her brother’s conviction and his consideration of the AI program. The stakes skyrocket.
  2. Invigorated by her find, Emily conducts a simulation. The results confirm that her old work could resolve the AI’s ethical pitfalls. Yet, the news about her brother gnaws at her; it’s no longer just an ethical quandary but a personal one. She faces the dilemma of knowing her actions could directly impact her brother’s future.
  3. The euphoria of the discovery is short-lived. Emily grapples with the moral implications of her next steps. She now holds the power to either redeem the project that could affect her brother or take it down altogether. It’s a complex ethical maze, and her brother’s situation adds an extra layer of gravity.

Scene 11: A Choice of Consequence

An emotional moment where Emily debates whether to use this discovery or to remove herself from the project. (Word Count: 300)
  1. Emily discovers her old research that reveals the key to fixing the AI’s ethical issues. She is overjoyed, seeing a path to redeem the program and restore her reputation.
  2. Upon reflection, Emily realizes the technology could still be misused for unethical purposes. She debates whether to correct the AI or withdraw completely to avoid future harm. Emily agonizes over the decision, uncertain which choice is ethically right.
  3. In a moment of clarity, Emily knows the ethical thing is to dismantle the flawed system, even at great personal cost. She must reveal the truth and prevent further ethical transgressions, regardless of the consequences she will face. Emily steels herself for the rocky road ahead.

Resolution

With her brother’s future hanging in the balance, Emily makes the irrevocable decision to expose the project’s ethical shortcomings, sacrificing her academic reputation. Her actions create a ripple effect that ends with an unexpected offer from a UN ethical AI oversight committee.

Scene 12: A Sister’s Burden

Emily exposes the AI’s ethical failures, facing both personal and professional consequences, while also dealing with the immediate impact on her brother.
  1. Emily consults with her brother one final time before making her decision. He’s conflicted, uncertain about his own future, but he trusts her judgment. With the weight of his life in her mind, Emily decides to shut down the AI program, preparing for the emotional and ethical fallout.
  2. Emily calls a press conference to expose the program’s ethical flaws. The public reacts with both praise and vitriol. David feels betrayed and points the finger at Emily. Her brother, watching from home, is filled with a complex mix of pride and apprehension about his own future.
  3. Legal threats and career-ending possibilities loom, but Emily releases a detailed paper outlining the program’s ethical inadequacies. Despite the backlash, she stands strong, committed to her moral principles. Emily’s brother contacts her, torn but ultimately supportive, realizing that she did what she believed was right.

Scene 13: The Price of Integrity

Emily navigates the rocky aftermath of her decision, foregoing a prestigious UN position to remain true to her ethical stance and to stay present for her brother.
  1. Emily is alienated by her peers. She faces not just professional isolation but also the burden of knowing her decision has thrown her brother’s life into uncertainty. Her academic and social reputations are in shambles.
  2. Amid the chaos, Emily learns that the rehabilitated ex-convicts have taken up the ethical mantle, revising the startup with a new focus. It’s a small consolation. Her brother suggests he might join them, leading Emily to ponder a difficult UN job offer that could distance her from her family and her grassroots impact.
  3. Emily, after soul-searching, declines the UN job. She wishes to continue her work on a community level and stay connected with her brother’s journey. Her advisor thinks she’s throwing away a golden opportunity, but Emily is steadfast. Her moral integrity remains intact, and her brother’s newfound hope in the revised startup adds a note of optimism to her difficult journey.
