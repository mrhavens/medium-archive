Shear Deception: The Wolf in Sheep’s Clothing

Another retelling of a classic fable for an equally special and beloved modern audience

When a cunning wolf infiltrates a flock of sheep by disguising itself as one of them, a brave lamb discovers its true identity and leads the flock in outwitting the deceitful predator.
