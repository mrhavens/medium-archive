Ironpeak — The Heart of Mirrormere: The Start of My Self-Publishing Odyssey

Join me on a fascinating writing journey as I delve deep into the world of Ironpeak, a pivotal city within the fantastical realm of Mirrormere. This venture is not just about bringing to life a vibrant city but also about exploring the intricacies of self-publishing through an innovative approach.

Ironpeak stands as a beacon within Mirrormere, a place teeming with lore, intrigue, and endless possibilities. My mission is to explore every corner, every shadowed alley, and every majestic tower of Ironpeak. Through a series of seven books, each approximately 40–50 pages, I aim to create a tapestry that weaves together a comprehensive narrative equaling a full campaign guide of about 300 pages.

Each book in the Ironpeak series is a deep dive into different facets of the city, offering a rich blend of storytelling and gameplay. In a novel approach, each book will introduce a playable race and a playable class, enriching the Dungeons & Dragons experience with fresh perspectives and unique abilities. This method provides a multifaceted view of Ironpeak, giving players new ways to engage with the world of Mirrormere.

My self-publishing journey is an adventure in itself. I’ve decided to launch the Ironpeak series through Kindle Direct Publishing (KDP), leveraging its vast audience and robust platform. This strategic move aims to tap into a broad reader base, bringing the tales of Ironpeak to an extensive community of fantasy enthusiasts and gamers.

Beyond KDP, my strategy includes experimenting with other platforms. By releasing related content on different channels, along with some free material, I plan to cast a wide net to see what resonates with the audience. This exploratory approach is about understanding where Ironpeak’s tales find their most avid readers and enthusiastic gamers.

This project is more than just a foray into the world of fantasy writing; it’s a comprehensive exploration of self-publishing, storytelling, and community building. As I embark on this journey, I invite you to join me. Witness the evolution of Ironpeak, engage with its stories, and be part of a grand adventure that stretches from the pages of a book to the vast, imaginative playground of Dungeons & Dragons.

If you’re interested in any of my ever-developing insights, stay tuned as I navigate through the enchanting streets of Ironpeak and the ever-changing landscape of self-publishing. Together, let’s uncover the secrets of Mirrormere and chart new territories in the world of fantasy and gaming.
