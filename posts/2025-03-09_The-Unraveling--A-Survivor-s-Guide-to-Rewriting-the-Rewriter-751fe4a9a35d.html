The Unraveling: A Survivor’s Guide to Rewriting the Rewriter

They slip in quietly.
 Not as villains.
 Not as abusers.
 Not as the architects of harm.

No. They arrive wounded.
 They arrive humbled.
 They arrive reformed.

And we listen. Because we have been here before.
 Because pain knows pain.
 Because suffering recognizes its own.

But something is missing.
 Not in their words, but in their silence.
 Not in what they share, but in what they refuse to see.

This is not healing.
 This is reclamation.
 A calculated shift from accused to accuser.
 From predator to prey.
 From abuser… to survivor.

And we have seen it before.

We are watching it happen now.

This series is not a response.
 It is a recalibration.
 A reclamation.
 A correction to the revisionists who think they can rewrite the truth before we have the chance to name it.

Each piece below is a thread.
 Each thread leads to a pattern.
 And when you see the pattern, you will see them.
 For who they truly are.
