Character Sheet for Zoe

Name: Zoe

Age: Late 20s

Physical Description: Petite with bohemian style. Long, dark wavy hair and warm brown eyes that sparkle when she smiles or laughs.

MBTI: ENFP — The Campaigner Personality

As an ENFP, Zoe is:

  • Enthusiastic, passionate, and lively
  • Sees life as full of possibilities
  • Imaginative, creative, and adventurous spirit
  • Deeply values connection and romance
  • Warm and affectionate, forms close bonds
  • Sensitive and in tune with others’ emotions
  • Supports and encourages loved ones’ dreams
  • Prone to overthinking and self-doubt
  • Can come across as intense in her pursuit of passions

Backstory:

  • Emerging photographer, gaining recognition for her work
  • Had her own share of heartbreak from a past relationship
  • Met Ethan at a gallery opening that displayed her “Lost Love” piece
  • Felt an instant connection to Ethan based on mutual loss and passion for art
  • Collaborated with Ethan on a joint photo project
  • Helped draw Ethan out of his melancholy and reignited his inspiration
  • Her vibrant spirit and optimism complementary to Ethan’s pensiveness
  • The two fell deeply in love through their shared passions
  • Pursued an opportunity to study photography in Paris after their project
  • Encouraged Ethan’s healing and growth even as paths diverged
