Divorcing a Narcissist: The Pigeon Chess Conundrum

Navigating the Emotional Minefield of Narcissistic Divorce

Imagine trying to play chess with a pigeon — a creature that refuses to follow the rules, creates chaos on the board, and still insists on claiming victory. This frustrating and seemingly impossible scenario is what it’s like to divorce a narcissist, a complex and emotionally draining experience that demands a unique approach for a successful outcome.
