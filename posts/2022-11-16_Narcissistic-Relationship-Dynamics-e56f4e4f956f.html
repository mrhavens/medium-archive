NEUTRALIZING NARCISSISM

Narcissistic Relationship Dynamics

Narcissists are manipulative people who use their partners to their advantage. Narcissists don’t care about loving other people or sharing their feelings. Instead, they will use you to fuel their ego. They will stop caring about you and abandon you.

People with narcissistic tendencies will often use emotion to manipulate those around them. They will do whatever they can to suppress or manipulate their victims’ feelings, especially during the initial stages of their relationship.

As with drug dealers, narcissists use “Love Bombing” to make their victims addicted to them to control their behavior.

Narcissism can cause rapid changes in a relationship. Healthy couples share many things, but a narcissist will tell lies to speed up the relationship.

To strengthen their bond, the narcissist might even pretend to be vulnerable with their target if doing so gives them an advantage.

Narcissists see the world in extremes. They are averse to listening to others and will make others feel bad for disagreeing with them.

Narcissists can also be highly competitive and look for ways to gain an advantage in any situation. If they believe it will make their partner look bad to make themselves look good, a narcissist will go to great lengths to make any relationship difficult.

They will look for people who will pay attention to them and validate their notion of reality. These people are known as their “Narcissistic Supply.” A narcissist may use only use one person as their supply at a time, or they may use many. But they will always discard them once the narcissist becomes bored or loses their supply’s loyalty and trust.

They will quickly find someone else to entertain them.

Ultimately, it is important to remember that narcissists seek someone to provide them with a narcissistic supply. Healthy relationships are built on trust and openness, and it is important to recognize the signs of an unhealthy relationship to protect yourself from being used as a narcissistic supply.
